Thrust Vector Control System
Attitude Control · May 2026 – Present

Implemented real-time 2-axis attitude estimation using an MPU-6050 IMU with a complementary filter for sensor fusion of accelerometer and gyroscope data. Built a closed-loop PID servo actuation system that responds to live IMU feedback at 100Hz on an Arduino UNO. Currently designing the 2-axis gimbal mechanism — 3D printed PETG rings with a machined aluminum frame — for active motor mount stabilization.

Active
Adaptive Cruise Control Testbed
Embedded Control · Apr 2026 – Present

Built an Arduino-driven real-time obstacle detection pipeline using ultrasonic sensing with a three-zone proximity response — cruise above 30cm, deceleration between 15–30cm, and full brake stop under 15cm — with proportional servo-actuated braking throughout. Currently designing a custom chassis and enclosure in SolidWorks for FDM fabrication and machined aluminum components, with manufacturability in mind from the start.

Active
Vacuum Chamber
Pressure Systems · Mar 2026 – Present

Designing a sub-chamber to characterize black powder initiator performance at simulated high-altitude pressures. Sized the structure for energetic loads up to 5g BP with a safety factor of 5, and incorporated a pressure-rated transducer port and O-ring face-seal for real-time pressure–time data acquisition. Currently refining the SolidWorks models so they're ready to be machined.
